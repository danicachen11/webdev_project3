Big Ben is a famous clock and bell located at the north end of the Palace of Westminster in London. While many people use the name to refer to the entire clock tower, it originally refers to the massive bell inside. Completed in 1859, Big Ben has become a symbol of London and the United Kingdom, known for its reliability, historic charm, and deep, resonant chimes that mark the passage of time.
